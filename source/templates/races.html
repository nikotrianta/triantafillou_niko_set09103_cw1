Races

As outlined in the Alliance and Horde dedicated faction descriptions, many races exist within the World of Warcraft.

Each race has a unique model and inherent abilities, where usually a class defines what role and combat proficiency can be used, races are unaffected by your class choices outside of class-race exclusives (such as Alliance Paladins can only be Humans, Dwarves, or Draenei, and Horde Paladins can only be Blood Elf or Tauren).
