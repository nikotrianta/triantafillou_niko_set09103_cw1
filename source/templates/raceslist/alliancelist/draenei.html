Draenei

Driven from their homeworld of Argus, the honourable Draenei fled the Burning Legion for eons before finding a remote planet to settle on. They shared this world with the shamanistic orcs and named it Draenor. In time the Legion corrupted the Orcs, who waged war and nearly exterminated the peaceful Draenei. A lucky few fled to Azeroth, where they now seek allies in their battle against the Burning Legion.

Playable Classes: Paladin / Monk / Priest / Shaman / Mage / Hunter / Warrior / Death Knight
