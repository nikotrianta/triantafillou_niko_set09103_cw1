Humans

Humans are a young race, and thus highly versatile, mastering the arts of combat, craftmanship and magic with stunning efficiency. The humans' valor and optimism have led them to build some of the world's greatest kingdoms. In this troubled era, after generations of conflict, humanity seeks to rekindle its former glory and forge a shining new future.

Playable Classes: Warrior / Paladin / Hunter / Rogue / Priest / Mage / Warlock / Monk / Death Knight
