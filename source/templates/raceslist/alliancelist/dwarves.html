Dwarves

In ages past, the dwarves cared only for riches taken from the earth's depths. Then records surfaced of a god-like race said to have given the dwarves life... and an enchanted birthright. Driven to learn more, the dwarves devoted themselves to the pursuit of lost artifacts and ancient knowledge. Today the dwarven archaeologists are scattered throughout the globe.

Playable Classes: Paladin / Monk / Warlock / Rogue / Priest / Shaman / Mage / Hunter / Warrior / Death Knight
