Tauren

Always the Tauren strive to preserve the balance of nature and heed the will of their Goddess, the Earth Mother. many of the nomadic wandering tribes of the Tauren have gathered together under a single banner to settle in the fertile plains of Mulgore. Were it not for the timely intervention of the Orcs, the whole race may have been wiped out by marauding centaur. The Tauren honour their blood-debt to this day, fighting alongisde the Horde to protect their land.

Playable Classes: Paladin / Monk / Priest / Shaman / Hunter / Warrior / Druid / Death Knight
