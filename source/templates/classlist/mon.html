Monk

Monks are known for their skill in hand-to-hand fighting, relying on their fists and feet as much as their weapons. Monks can also specialise in calling upon the restorative power of the mists to heal allies.

Role: Tank, Healer or Melee Damage

Aseko

Aseko is the name of the Monk character that I currently play when a little bit more of diversity is required for my Guild's raids. Monks excell in healing where Paladins cannot, and so this characters value is quite favoured for certain encounters.
