Paladin

Paladins are heavily-armoured fighters and defenders who use Holy magic to heal wounds and combat evil. They can focus on two-handed weapons, shields or healing.

Role: Tank, Healer or Melee Damage

Akesia

Akesia is the name of the Paladin that I currently play, and is the main character I use to raid with. Akesia's main combat role is Healer, which means that I use her avatar to ensure that the other players in my raid group are kept alive throughout the boss encounters my Guild and I play through. If anyone's avatar dies during combat, this is a direct reflection on my part!
