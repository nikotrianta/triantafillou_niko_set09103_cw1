Alliance

For the Alliance!

The Alliance is a collective union of races found throughout Azeroth and is comprised of Humans, Night Elves, Dwarves, Gnomes, Draenei and Worgen. They embody pride and nobility, alongside courage and wisdom, and this union work to maintain order throughout the world, as it is driven by honour and tradition.
