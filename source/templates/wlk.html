Warlock

Warlocks cast Fire or Shadow magic to damage, dran, or curse their enemy. They summon demons as servants.

Role: Ranged Damage

Asekias

Asekias is the name of the Warlock I currently play when I am not needed on my Paladin character. Whilst Warlocks are pigeon-holed into one combat role of ranged damage, they come with a myriad of utility, such as summoning portals to transport group or raid members, and can create a soulstone that allows for a group or raid member to be resurrected during combat!
